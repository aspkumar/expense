Expense Sheet

This simple expense sheet relies on several technolgies. Primarily, local storage is essential to maintain data from session to session. Most modern browsers now support this technology.

To use:

  • Name your expense
  • Add a value (whole number or decimal format) into either the expense or column field (not both)
  • Pick a date
  • Commit the entry into the database

Feel free to try this app on both your desktop, laptop, tablet, or phone! It should scale accordingly

Also, try sorting columns by selecting the column header
